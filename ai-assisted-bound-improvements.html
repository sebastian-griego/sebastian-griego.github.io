Six AI-assisted bound improvements for optimization constants

In May 2026, I ran an experiment in LLM-assisted mathematical discovery using GPT-5.5 Pro in ChatGPT as part of a human-checked workflow. The target was the Optimization Constants in Mathematics repository, a curated collection of best known bounds for mathematical constants. The experiment produced six merged bound-improvement certificates for entries 3a, 3c, 10c, 42, 45, and 59.

The repository notes that submissions are reviewed for minimal plausibility and replicability, but are not certified by the site for correctness. I use "merged" below in that sense: these are reviewable certificates and computations, not a claim of independent theorem certification by the repository.
